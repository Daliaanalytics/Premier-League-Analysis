# Premier League Analysis ⚽

## Overview
Analysis of Premier League players statistics using Python and Power BI.

## Dataset
- 580 players
- 34 columns
- Source: FBref

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Power BI

## Analysis
- Top 10 Scorers
- Top 10 Assists
- Top 10 Goals + Assists
- Total Goals by Team
- Players Distribution by Team
- Nationalities Distribution
- Age Distribution
- Top Players by Minutes Played
- Actual Goals vs Expected Goals (xG)
- Top Players by Progressive Carries

## Dashboard
Power BI dashboard with 3 pages:
- Overview
- Players Analysis
- Performance

## Key Findings
- Erling Haaland is the top scorer with 27 goals
- Cole Palmer leads in Goals + Assists with 33 G+A
- Ollie Watkins is the top assist provider with 13 assists
- Manchester City scored the most goals with 94 goals
- Sheffield United scored the least with 31 goals
- 45.5% of players are English
- Most players are aged between 24-25 years
- Jeremy Doku leads in Progressive Carries with 200+
- Manchester City has the fewest players (25) but scored the most goals
